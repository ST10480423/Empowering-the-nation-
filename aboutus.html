About Empowering the Nation

Transforming lives through accessible, practical education that empowers domestic workers and gardeners to build better futures for themselves and their families.

About Us

We help domestic workers and gardeners learn new skills .These skills can help them get better jobs,earn more money, or start their own small business.Our founder, Precious Radebe, started this training to give people in the community more chances in life. she believes everyone should have the tools to succeed.

We keep our training simple, practical, and useful. By helping one person at a time, we are building a stronger community by providing resources, creating a supportive environment where individuals can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Mission

At Empowering the Nation, we're dedicated to breaking down barriers to professional development. We understand that many hardworking individuals in domestic and gardening services lack access to quality training programs that could advance their careers.

Our Foundation Practical Rebirth program provides comprehensive, hands-on training that leads to industry certification, better employment opportunities, higher wages, and the confidence to start independent businesses.

Our Impact

Since 2020, we've trained over 500 individuals, with 95% finding better employment or starting their own businesses within 6 months of graduation.

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do and shape how we serve our community.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering individuals with skills that create real opportunities for career advancement and financial independence.

Accessibility

Quality education should be accessible to everyone. That's why all our courses are completely free of charge.

Practical Learning

Our hands-on approach ensures students gain real-world skills they can immediately apply in their careers.

Community Support

We build a supportive community where learners can connect, share experiences, and grow together.
